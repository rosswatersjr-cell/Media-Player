YouTube Downloader Readme    

This Program Downloads Video And Audio Files From YouTube.com. 
To Download, Your Browser Cookies Must Be Enabled And Your VPN 
Turned Off. To Download A Single File, Play A Desired Video Or 
Audio File. Below The Video Play Window Select 'Share' And Then 
Select 'Copy'. Once Copied, Pause Or Close YouTube. To Download 
A Play List, Select 'View Full Play And Then Select 'Share' And 
Then Select 'Copy'. Use Right-Click 'Paste' Or Keys 'ctrl+v'To 
Copy The URL To The Entry Field And Then Click 'Download'. Follow 
The Instructions To Complete The Download. Never Copy From The 
Address Bar Because The Video Or Audio May Be Part Of A Play List 
Which Includes The Complete Play List.
